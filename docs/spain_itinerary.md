- **Afternoon:** easy settle-in. A slow lunch near the hotel, a first wander. Don't over-program — you've just flown.

### Sunday June 14 — Slow organic-market day (together)

  - **Mercado de San Antón** (Chueca) — covered, every day, rooftop terrace.

- **Midday:** long market lunch.
- **Afternoon:** Retiro Park in the shade, or siesta.
- **Optional museum (if energy):** Sunday Prado free window is **17:00–19:00** but it's the *busiest* slot (60–90 min queues). Better to save museums for the calmer weekday evenings. If you want one now, **Reina Sofía** (Sun afternoon free) is the lighter choice.
